# Active Management Status Vocabulary

## Why this exists
The committee needs stable language so that status changes have operational meaning.

## Thesis states
- `pending`: no formal thesis yet
- `active`: thesis documented and intact
- `under-review`: evidence changed enough to require review
- `threatened`: core thesis is under material pressure
- `exited`: position is no longer active

## CIO actions
- `build-now`: increase or initiate now
- `hold`: keep current sizing
- `trim`: reduce position
- `exit`: close position
- `watch-trigger`: monitor until a condition occurs
- `preserve-cash`: avoid deploying capital here for now
- `n-a`: not applicable

## Monitoring priority
- `P1`: immediate/high-value monitoring
- `P2`: normal active monitoring
- `P3`: low-intensity monitoring

## Role labels
- `core`: central long-term position
- `watchlist`: smaller or not yet fully proven position
- `tactical`: shorter-horizon asymmetric position
- `hedge`: intentional offset / protection
- `liquidity`: cash or cash-equivalent
- `special`: structure with specific legal / liquidity / wrapper behavior
