# Validation Checklist — Research Outputs

## Goal
Ensure Sunday and command-driven research outputs are relevant, grounded, and useful for portfolio decisions.

## Source Hygiene Checks
- every factual claim comes from knowledge base or explicit fresh source
- stale or missing source coverage is declared
- no unsupported numeric claim is presented as fact

## Portfolio Relevance Checks
- current positions are prioritized over generic market commentary
- every major section explains impact on the portfolio where relevant
- no analyst block is included without real change or live relevance

## Thesis Consistency Checks
- output must not contradict stored thesis status without explanation
- if thesis pressure increases, state why
- if thesis is unchanged, avoid fake novelty

## Output Quality Checks
- summary is concise and readable in Discord
- appendix contains deeper detail without duplicating summary verbatim
- uncertainty is explicit
- no markdown tables

## Failure Policy
- if research source coverage is partial, note exact stale areas
- if portfolio state is stale, research output must warn that downstream interpretation may be degraded
